Welcome to Huts & Hats

A place away from the hustle of the city where the first ray of sun couldn't be more pretty. A prominent wildlife destination, to get a glimpse of the wild living free. A place where you can enjoy the rustling of leaves on trees with tweets and breeze. A place of saying ‘I did’, ‘I do’, and ‘I will continue to be'. A hideout with complete tranquility!

16kms uphill from the city of lakes- Nainital, at the height of 7,510 ft above sea level. Huts and Hats wildlife destination houses 580 different species of birds. With leopards calling and tigers roaming free, it's the perfect place to live in nature's hierarchy.

The immense wildlife and the landscape amidst our location is, “Cherry on the cake”. While the drive to us, passes through the forested Echo Zone with a splendid view of the Great Himalayas, Mt. Nanda Devi around The Naina Peak Range.

Shooting Range : A day spent wasting bullets is never a day wasted! Huts and Hats offers a safe, well equipped and professionally run shooting range as recreational activity for those who dare. With air rifle and pistol targets, it’s a game of patience. Learning when to breathe and when to stop, sharpening your shooting skills with every miss, gazing as the light pours in through the heart of the target, till you hit the bullseye.

Night & Day Safari : Pack your bags for the epic and magical endeavours, allowing you to sneak a peek at some of the most majestic and wonderful flora and fauna. Huts and Hats offers both night and day safari for visitors to experience, admire, observe, learn and photograph the wildlife from the wild itself.

Camping : Camping is all about cold air, dark night, warm fire, and bright stars! Huts & Hats offers overnight camps for the ones who would like to smell the fresh oak trees and crickets at night when you sleep. Where social networking takes place around a bonfire with music and tea, where food cooked and consumed in open air makes it taste better infinitely.

Trekking / Birds Watching : When the mountains are calling you must go! Huts & Hats offer a well planned trek in nature to witness a thousand miracles and the best views that come after the hardest climbs. A journey with one foot in front of the other again and again, allows you to witness beauty throughout the entirety of the trek in every step you take & Many More.
